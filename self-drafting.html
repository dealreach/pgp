Prompt #11: Obstacle Framework
OBSTACLE CHAPTER FRAMEWORK (MANDATORY) 1. Introduction without a title, including a natural transition statement 2. Describe the obstacle and its impact - Emotional impact - Practical impact - Long-term consequence - Hidden damage caused by the obstacle 3. Explain how, when, and why this obstacle appears Reasons behind it: - Lack of awareness or understanding - Lack of practical skills - Lack of emotional resilience - Wrong mindset or belief system - Social pressure or environmental influence - Emotional conditioning and self-sabotaging patterns 4. Show relatable real-life creator behaviours or scenarios - Common beginner reactions - Thought patterns caused by the obstacle - How creators unknowingly make the situation worse - Daily-life creator situations where this obstacle appears 5. Introduce the solution - Emotional breakthrough - Perspective shift - Initial mindset correction 6. Explain the solution deeply - Psychology behind the solution - Emotional regulation required - Behaviour changes required - New mindset required - Skills needed to overcome the obstacle 7. Show the step-by-step implementation process - Practical actions - Behavioural adjustments - Habits and exercises - Recovery systems - Progress checkpoints 8. End with an untitled conclusion - Reflection - Emotional realization - Encouragement - Transition to the next stage of growth
Prompt #12: Evolution Framework
EVOLUTION CHAPTER FRAMEWORK (MANDATORY) 1. Introduction without a title, including a natural transition statement 2. Describe the current identity problem or limitation - How it limits creator growth - Emotional and psychological consequence - Why most creators stay stuck at this level 3. Explain how and why this limitation develops Reasons behind it: - Lack of self-awareness - Lack of identity clarity - Copying others instead of understanding oneself - Fear of being authentic - Lack of long-term creator thinking - Social validation dependency 4. Show relatable creator behaviours or identity patterns - Common beginner identity mistakes - How creators lose originality - Behaviour caused by insecurity and imitation - Real-life creator mindset examples 5. Introduce the evolved perspective or identity shift 6. Explain the transformation deeply - Psychology behind creator identity - Authenticity and audience connection - Perspective shifts required - Emotional maturity required - Long-term creator evolution 7. Show the step-by-step identity development process - Self-discovery exercises - Reflection practices - Perspective-building habits - Identity alignment actions - Long-term growth checkpoints 8. End with an untitled conclusion - Reflection - Identity realization - Emotional closure - Natural transition toward deeper creator evolution
