Instruction for Elegance

Luxury Execution Guidelines

Video
MANDATORY: The idea must feel truly non-generic — distinct from and elevated above common modern audiovisual creative trends. It must set a new benchmark in luxury audiovisual storytelling. Every creative decision must reflect refined elegance, intentionality, and premium aesthetic sensibility, including the presentation angle, scene selection, people, locations, objects, wardrobe, colors, lighting, framing, pacing, dialogue, voice delivery, sound design, and overall emotional atmosphere. The creative must avoid loudness, exaggeration, visual clutter, cheap commercial energy, or trend-chasing aesthetics. The execution should remain minimal, effortless, and deeply sophisticated, while the overall impact must feel emotionally powerful, memorable, psychologically compelling, and naturally conversion-driven — crafted specifically for an aristocratic, highly affluent audience.
Cinematic Image
MANDATORY: The visual direction must feel truly non-generic — distinct from and elevated above common modern luxury visual trends. Every creative decision must reflect refined elegance, intentionality, restraint, and premium aesthetic sensibility, including the composition, framing, presentation angle, subject selection, people, locations, wardrobe, objects, materials, textures, colors, lighting, depth, negative space, and overall cinematic atmosphere. The image must avoid loudness, exaggeration, oversaturation, visual clutter, cheap commercial aesthetics, artificial-looking glamour, or trend-chasing social media styling. The execution should feel minimal, effortless, timeless, and deeply sophisticated, crafted specifically for an aristocratic, highly affluent audience.
