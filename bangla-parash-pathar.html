Prompt #1
How To Create and Set Up A [App or Way]?

Please write the answer in the most spontaneous and natural sounding modern, everyday conversational Bengali as spoken in Kolkata, West Bengal, India that can be understood by an 21 year old but contains the international standards of language.

Strict Rules:

Must subtly reflect Kolkata conversational cadence through sentence rhythm and phrasing by using slightly formal, polite, and smoothly flowing sentence structures, with balanced pacing and no abrupt or overly casual wording — without slang, regional grammar, or loss of conservative authority.

• Always use the formal “আপনি”, not the informal “তুমি.” The address is formal, but the tone should remain warm, conversational, and approachable.
• The voice should be bold, direct, also should feel like a close friend and a trusted authority, speaking one-on-one with the reader in a natural, face-to-style.
