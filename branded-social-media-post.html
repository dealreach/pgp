Prompt #2: Visual Direction
Please create a high-converting 1:1 image as the feature image of this social media post RULE (MANDATORY) Create a premium luxury-minimalist visual with the emotional identity: “Quiet luxury meets thoughtful modern promotion.” Use a refined warm luxury palette featuring champagne gold (#C89B3C), metallic bronze (#8B5E1A), soft ivory (#F8F4EC), elegant cream white (#FFFDF8), muted premium browns (#35271A), and sophisticated warm neutral tones. The visual style should feel calm, sophisticated, trustworthy, editorial, cinematic, and high-end — without appearing loud, flashy, or overly commercial. Incorporate subtle metallic gold reflections, soft cinematic lighting, warm ambient glow, luxury gradients, refined glassmorphism, elegant depth, and clean minimalist composition. Style direction: modern luxury branding, elegant editorial aesthetics, premium lifestyle visuals, tasteful product presentation, refined UI design, cinematic warmth, sophisticated minimalism, polished luxury atmosphere, and human-first visual communication. Maintain a premium “quiet luxury” feel with balanced whitespace, restrained elegance, soft contrast, and visually soothing composition. Avoid: oversaturated colors, cluttered layouts, aggressive advertising aesthetics, neon tones, cheap-looking gold textures, harsh shadows, cyberpunk styles, cartoonish elements, glossy plastic surfaces, or flashy luxury clichés.
