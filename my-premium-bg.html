The LED lighting reflects softly off the desk surface, adding depth and warmth. Shot from a side-angle perspective with a 35mm lens, f/1.8 aperture, ISO 400, shallow depth of field, soft cinematic bokeh, crisp focus on the subject’s face and hands, realistic skin texture, gentle shadows, balanced highlights, modern editorial photography, ultra-high resolution, cozy nighttime influencer aesthetic.
Prompt #2: Composition Mapping
COMPOSITION & POSITIONING (MANDATORY) Reposition the woman to the extreme left side of the canvas. Her body must be aligned very close to the left border of the frame, leaving only a tiny natural margin. The character must occupy the bottom-left area of the composition. Remove the large empty space between the woman and the left edge of the image. Keep the large negative/blank space on the RIGHT side of the canvas. Do not crop the body. Do not change the woman, saree, pose, expression, lighting, background texture, decorative wave lines, color palette, scale, or overall visual style. Only change the character placement/composition. The final composition should visually feel right-weighted, not center-weighted.
